Variants:

-->The Audi Q5 facelift is available in two variants including Premium Plus and Technology.

Market introduction:

-->The Audi Q5 facelift was launched in India on 23 November, 2021.

Engine and specification:

-->The facelifted Audi Q5 is propelled by a 2.0-litre, four-cylinder, TFSI petrol engine that produces 245bhp and 370Nm of torque. Paired to a seven-speed automatic unit, power is sent to all four wheels via the Quattro all-wheel-drive system.

Exterior design:

-->The 2021 Audi Q5 gets a new single-frame grille, new LED headlamps, revised front and rear bumpers, tweaked LED tail lights, and a set of new 19-inch alloy wheels.

Interior and features:

-->Inside, the updated Audi Q5 facelift receives features in the form of eight airbags, Audi Virtual Cockpit Plus (fully digital instrument console), Audi Park Assist, a 10.1-inch MMI Plus infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, a panoramic sunroof, wireless charger, cruise control, ambient lighting, electrically adjustable front seats, a new steering wheel, and an electronically-operated tail-gate.

Colours:

-->The new Audi Q5 is offered in five colours including Navarra Blue Metallic, Ibis White Solid, Floret Silver Metallic, Mythos Black Metallic, and Manhattan Gray Metallic.

Seating capacity:

-->The Audi Q5 facelift has a seating capacity of five occupants.
